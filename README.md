1.16.1 Boat Clutch practice map - Short demonstration video: https://youtu.be/DAjeBqQhnkM

For a specific height, click the according sign. For a random height use the random height sign.

To respawn switch to your 6 slot.

If you die/respawn after placing/dropping your boat you can press the green sign to remove all placed/dropped boats and receive a new one.

When quickly respawning and jumping off again be aware of minecrafts 3 second immunity window after respawning. So for lower heights wait a short moment before jumping again.

Feel free to go in creative mode and adjust the hotbar to your liking.
